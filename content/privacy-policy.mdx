## 1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### a. Information Automatically Collected

We generally do not collect personal information directly, except when you voluntarily provide it (for example, by contacting us or subscribing to updates). However, third-party services integrated into our website may collect data when you visit. This includes:

- **Cookies and Tracking Technologies**: Third-party services such as Google AdSense and Google Analytics use cookies to enhance your experience, analyze site traffic, and serve personalized content. These cookies may track your browsing habits to deliver personalized ads. You can control cookies through your browser settings or our cookie consent banner.
- **Log File Information**: Third-party tools may collect log files that include IP addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, and date/time stamps.
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics and similar tools help measure traffic and usage trends for the website.
- **Device Information**: Information collected from your browser, such as operating system and type of device, is used to optimize user experience. This data is not relatable to personal information.
- **IP Address**: Your IP address may be collected by third-party services to determine your general location for audience analytics and advertiser insights.
- **Identifiers**: Anonymous identifiers may be assigned to track activity and interactions on the site, including transactions with partners for revenue tracking purposes.

### b. Information from APIs

Some of our tools and services may rely on third-party APIs to function. These APIs may collect and process data independently. We ensure that any APIs we use come from reputable providers with strong privacy standards.

### c. Information You Provide

If you sign up for our email newsletter or subscribe to updates, you may provide us with a username and email address. Passwords are not required; instead, a sign-in link will be sent to your email address.

If you complete forms to connect with our partners, the information you provide will be shared only with the partners indicated at the time of collection.
