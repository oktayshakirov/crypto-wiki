## Important Notice

**TheCrypto.Wiki uses affiliate links throughout this website.** This means that when you click on certain links (particularly links to cryptocurrency exchanges, wallets, and related services) and make a purchase or sign up for a service, we may receive a commission at no additional cost to you. This disclosure applies to all pages on our website.

### About TheCrypto.Wiki

TheCrypto.Wiki is an independent publisher dedicated to providing objective, educational, and informative content about cryptocurrency and blockchain technology. To support the operations and growth of our website, TheCrypto.Wiki partners with select organizations that may compensate us. These partnerships allow us to continue offering free resources to our audience.

### Partner Relationships and Affiliate Links

- **Affiliate Links Are Present**: Many pages on TheCrypto.Wiki include affiliate links, particularly on exchange review pages, guides about buying cryptocurrency, and pages featuring trading tools. When you click these links and complete a purchase or sign-up, we may earn a commission.
- **No Additional Cost to You**: Using our affiliate links does not increase the price you pay. The commission comes from the service provider, not from you.
- **Editorial Independence**: Compensation from our partners does not influence the integrity of our reviews, rankings, or comparisons. Partners cannot pay for favorable treatment, higher rankings, or positive reviews.
- **Research-Based Content**: All reviews and comparisons are conducted based on thorough research and, where applicable, actual use of the service or product.
- **Clear Disclosure**: Affiliate relationships are disclosed prominently on pages containing affiliate links through our disclaimer banner and near relevant links or sections to ensure transparency for readers.

The content on TheCrypto.Wiki is provided solely for informational and educational purposes. It does not constitute an offer to buy or sell any security, product, service, or investment. ### Transparency and Trust

**Editorial Independence**: Our editorial content is created independently of our commercial relationships. While we may receive compensation through affiliate programs, this does not determine which products or services we cover or how we evaluate them. We maintain complete editorial control over our content.

**Partner Information**: A list of our partners and affiliate programs will be made available upon request. Transparency is central to our mission, and we strive to provide unbiased, fact-based information for our audience.

**How to Identify Affiliate Links**: Affiliate links on our website are typically:

- Links to cryptocurrency exchanges (Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, etc.)
- Links to wallet providers and crypto services
- Links within exchange review pages
- Links in guides about buying or trading cryptocurrency

These links are clearly marked with our disclaimer banner on relevant pages, and individual affiliate links may include additional disclosure text.
