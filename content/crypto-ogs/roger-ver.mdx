Roger Ver, famously known as "Bitcoin Jesus," is globally recognized as the first major investor in Bitcoin startups. His support and advocacy significantly shaped the cryptocurrency landscape, backing pioneering companies such as [Bitcoin.com](https://www.bitcoin.com/), [Blockchain.com](https://www.blockchain.com/), [Ripple](https://ripple.com/), [BitPay](https://www.bitpay.com/) and [Kraken](/exchanges/kraken).

> "Bitcoin is one of the most important inventions in all of human history. For the first time ever, anyone can send or receive any amount of money with anyone else, anywhere on the planet, conveniently and without restriction." – Roger Ver

![Bitcoin Logo](/images/posts/bitcoin-neon.jpg)

## Early Life and Entrepreneurial Spirit

Roger Ver was born on January 27, 1979, in San Jose, California. An early entrepreneur, he demonstrated a knack for business and technology from a young age. After dropping out of De Anza College, he pursued his passion for business, quickly achieving success with MemoryDealers.com, an online computer hardware retailer specializing in high-quality networking equipment.

### Interesting Facts about Roger Ver

- **Early Bitcoin Advocate**: Roger became an early Bitcoin adopter in 2011 when the cryptocurrency was relatively unknown. His conviction in Bitcoin’s potential led him to invest heavily in multiple startups, significantly influencing their growth and the broader adoption of cryptocurrencies.
- **Voluntaryism Advocate**: He is a staunch supporter of Voluntaryism, advocating for a society based on voluntary transactions and interactions rather than governmental coercion.
- **Bitcoin Jesus**: Due to his persistent evangelism and promotion of Bitcoin, Roger earned the nickname "Bitcoin Jesus," symbolizing his significant role in popularizing Bitcoin globally.
- **Citizenship Renouncement**: Roger renounced his U.S. citizenship in 2014, becoming a citizen of Saint Kitts and Nevis, primarily for reasons aligned with his libertarian beliefs and pursuit of greater economic freedom.
- **Bitcoin Cash Proponent**: He played a pivotal role in promoting Bitcoin Cash (BCH), viewing it as a closer reflection of Bitcoin’s original purpose—peer-to-peer digital cash.
- **Controversies and Debates**: Roger is known for his fiery debates and public disagreements within the cryptocurrency community, particularly regarding the direction of Bitcoin and Bitcoin Cash, often sparking heated discussions.

## Investment Portfolio and Influence

Roger Ver's early investments became foundational in the cryptocurrency space. His foresight and financial backing provided critical early-stage support for:

- **Bitcoin.com**: A major hub for Bitcoin and cryptocurrency news, wallets, and educational resources.
- **Blockchain.com**: One of the most widely used Bitcoin wallets and blockchain explorers.
- **Ripple (XRP)**: A real-time gross settlement system aiming to facilitate cross-border payments.
- **BitPay**: A leading Bitcoin payment processing platform that simplifies transactions for businesses.
- **Kraken**: One of the world's largest cryptocurrency exchanges known for its security and broad asset support.

## Bitcoin Cash and Advocacy for Crypto Freedom

Roger Ver was instrumental in the creation and advocacy of Bitcoin Cash, a Bitcoin fork launched in 2017 aiming to address scalability issues with larger block sizes, thereby facilitating faster and cheaper transactions. Roger views Bitcoin Cash as a realization of Bitcoin’s original vision: a peer-to-peer electronic cash system accessible to everyone.

## Philosophy and Vision

Roger Ver is a vocal proponent of economic freedom, individual liberty, and minimal government intervention. His belief in cryptocurrencies as tools to empower individuals financially and socially has deeply influenced his investment decisions and public advocacy.

He has consistently emphasized the importance of cryptocurrency in countries facing economic challenges, arguing that digital currencies can offer financial independence and security in unstable regions.

> "Cryptocurrencies are the tools to separate money and state. They give the power back to the individual." – Roger Ver

## Humanitarian Efforts

Roger has also been involved in various humanitarian efforts, using cryptocurrency to support charitable causes and disaster relief. He notably supported charities like Free Ross Ulbricht, advocating for clemency for Ross Ulbricht, the creator of Silk Road.
